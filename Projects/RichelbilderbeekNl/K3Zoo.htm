In this song, K3 describes about living together with someone and owning a lot of animals. These animals are then used for houskeeping. The verses are neutral, the chorus as well.

 

Personally, I remove this song from my playlist immediatly: I can find no relevant nor fun lyrics. Sure, [1] can be interpreted as naughty, which makes it for me the best lyric in the song. [2] is in my opinion an attempt to use funny lyrics, but for me this fails miserably.
