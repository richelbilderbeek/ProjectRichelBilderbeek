(C++) Andrei Alexandrescu

 

 

Andrei Alexandrescu is one of the most important C++ people [1] and author of one of the most important C++ books [2].

 

 

 

 

 

List of books (co-)authored by Andrei Alexandrescu

 

  • Andrei Alexandrescu. Modern C++ Design. 2001. ISBN: 0201704315.
  • Herb Sutter, Andrei Alexandrescu. C++ coding standards: 101 rules, guidelines, and best practices. 2005. ISBN: 0-32-111358-6.

 

According to Scott Meyers, 'Modern C++ Design' is one of the most important C++ books [2].
