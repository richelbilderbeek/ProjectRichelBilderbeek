(Levend Cluedo) De Vermoorde Onderbuuf

 

Levend Cluedo 'De Vermoorde Onderbuuf' was written at the 11th and 12th of August 2009 by Mirte van Geffen and Richel Bilderbeek.
The opinions of the characters described do not reflect the authors' opinions. Any resemblance to persons living or dead is purely coincidental.
This Levend Cluedo script may be copied, modified and performed freely as long as the authors' names are mentioned. It is not allowed to distribute modified copies of the script without permission from the authors.

 

De Vermoorde Onderbuuf is a Levend Cluedo about the stereotypic student life about beer and (wo)men. The cast consists of (totally stupid) men that think derogatory about women and (totally stupid) women that think derogatory about men. Both sexes think mostly about beer and sex. This Levend Cluedo is suitable for people over 18 years old. Especially students enjoy this fun and parodic Levend Cluedo.
