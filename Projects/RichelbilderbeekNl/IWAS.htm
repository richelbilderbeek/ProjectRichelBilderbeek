IWAS

 

IWAS is an abbreviation of 'International Welding Appreciation Society' and is founded by me and Patrick Collopy in Bristol in June or July 2005.

 

At the 16th June of 2005 the founders watched a video about welding techniques. Twice! Since them, while walking in between pubs, all metal objects were inspected for welds. And nice welds were appreciated.

 

The first IWAS conference was kept in June or July 2005.
