(Levend Cluedo) Schoolfeest

 

Levend Cluedo 'Schoolfeest' was written by Jeroen Rozema and Richel Bilderbeek.
The opinions of the characters described do not reflect the authors' opinions. Any resemblance to persons living or dead is purely coincidental.
This Levend Cluedo script may be copied, modified and performed freely as long as the authors' names are mentioned. It is not allowed to distribute modified copies of the script without permission from the authors.

 

Schoolfeest is a Levend Cluedo situated at a secondary school in a small village. The (mostly stupid) cast consists of adolescents and their teaching staff. The script uses stereotypes of some cultures (including the Dutch) and contains some racism (albeit that the racistic youngsters are totally stupid).
