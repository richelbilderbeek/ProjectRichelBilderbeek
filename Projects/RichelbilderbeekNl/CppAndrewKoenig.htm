(C++) Andrew Koenig

 

Andrew Koenig is the C++ programmer that has Koenig Lookup named after him.

 

Andrew Koenig is one of the 5 most important C++ people [0].

 

 

 

 

 

List of books co-authored by Andrew Koenig

 

  • Andrew Koenig. C Traps and Pitfalls. 1988. ISBN: 0201179288
  • Andrew Koenig & Barbara Moo. Ruminations on C++. 1997. ISBN: 0-201-42339-1
  • Andrew Koenig & Barbara Moo. Accelerated C++. 2000. ISBN: 0-201-70353-X
