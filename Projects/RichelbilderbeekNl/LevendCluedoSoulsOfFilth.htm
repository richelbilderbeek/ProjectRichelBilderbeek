(Levend Cluedo) Souls Of Filth

 

Levend Cluedo 'Souls Of Filth' was written by Hester van Santen and Richel Bilderbeek in 2002.
The opinions of the characters described do not reflect the authors' opinions. Any resemblance to persons living or dead is purely coincidental.
This Levend Cluedo script may be copied, modified and performed freely as long as the authors' names are mentioned. It is not allowed to distribute modified copies of the script without permission from the authors.

 

Souls Of Filth is the most experimental Levend Cluedo, intended for an experienced crew of advanced Levend Cluedo players. Its main themes are blood, sex and (the stereotype of) gothic music. The (mostly stupid) characters are mad, sick, dark and/or unhealthy. This horror-Levend Cluedo is best played in an abandoned house at midnight, with adult and experienced players.

Setting 'Souls Of Filth'

 

Vanavond treed the band 'Souls of Filth' op, je favoriete band en jij bent er ook!

 

Maar...

 

Kees kwam naakt de kamer in kwam rennen vanuit de keuken en viel daar op de grond. 5 minuten ademde hij niet meer en had hij geen hartslag, tot hij wonder boven wonder weer wakker werd. Toen Kees wakker werd zei hij dat zijn vriendin Anita gestorven is in de keuken. Men ging er heen en trof daar het lijk aan van Anita, naakt en zonder bloed.
