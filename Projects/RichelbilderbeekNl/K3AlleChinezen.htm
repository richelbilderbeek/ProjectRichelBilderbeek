In this song, K3 states that Chinese people are (weird yet) okay, as well as all other people. The song has two moods: neutral verses and a neutral chorus. In the chorus there are backing vocals making (the sound of) karate moves.

 

Personally, I think the lyrics are uninspired and the karate moves as annoying. I am sorry for the Chinese being the theme of this song. Except for the karate moves, there is no reference made to Chinese traits nor culture. I expect this song was written just to fill space on a CD. I always delete this song from my playlists.
