(OpenFOAM) OpenFoamErrorOpenCellsFoundFixed

 

ToolOpenFoamErrorOpenCellsFoundFixed shows the solution of the OpenFOAM error ToolOpenFoamErrorOpenCellsFound.

 

The error was caused by not putting the internal faces first in the file 'boundary'.

I posted this solution at cfd-online.com its OpenFOAM forum.

 

Of the mesh I used, I made some pictures. The top-left image shows the point indices, the others the face indices.
