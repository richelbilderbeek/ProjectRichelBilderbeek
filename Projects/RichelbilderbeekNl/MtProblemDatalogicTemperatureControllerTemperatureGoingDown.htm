(Mechatronics) Datalogic temperature controller temparature is going down

 

Datalogic temperature controller temparature is going down is is a problem.

 

Hardware: Datalogic temperature controller ES-series

Software configuration:

  • AL21 10
  • AtOF
  • Pidd
  • P 20
  • i 120
  • d 30
  • t 20
  • Adt0
  • AL21
  • tcJ
  • U degrees C n
  • LSP -50
  • HSP 760
  • 0 degrees C
  • LFA0
  • LOC3

 

Observation: when starting the machine, the controller displayed the ambient temperature. But then, the temperature displayed decreased to -50 degrees Celsius, after which 'UnFL' ('Underflow') was displayed.

 

Cause: the two-wire resistance thermometer is connected to the backside of the controller in the backwards order.

 

Solution: swap the wires connected to connectors 14 and 15.
