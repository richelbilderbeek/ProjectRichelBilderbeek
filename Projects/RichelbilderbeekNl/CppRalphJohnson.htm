(C++) Ralph Johnson

 

Ralph Johnson is one of 'The Gang Of Four' that wrote 'Design Patterns' [1], which is/was one of the most important C++ books [2].

 

 

 

 

 

List of books co-authored by Ralph Johnson

 

  • Erich Gamma, Richard Helm, Ralph Johnson, John Vlissides. Design Patterns. 1995. ISBN: 0201633612.
