(Teaching) Competency

 

In the competency-based view of teacher-education in teaching theory, a teacher is defined as a good teacher when able to satisfy all needed competencies.

 

 

 

 

 

Competencies in The Netherlands

 

In The Netherlands, seven competencies are defined each teacher must be compentent in. This can be self-scored by using QuickScan.

 

These are (from [1] in same ordering):

  1. Interpersonal
  2. Pedagogical
  3. Didactical
  4. Organizational
  5. Cooperation with collegues
  6. Cooperation with other professionals
  7. Reflection and development
