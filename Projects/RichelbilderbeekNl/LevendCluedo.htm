Levend Cluedo

 

This is my page about Levend Cluedo.

 

The opinions of the characters described in any Levend Cluedo script do not reflect the opinions of the author(s). Any resemblance to persons living or dead is purely coincidental.
All Levend Cluedo scripts may be copied, modified and performed freely as long as the names of the author(s) are mentioned. It is not allowed to distribute modified copies of the script without permission from the author(s).

 

Levend Cluedo is an improvised theatre-play in which every actor gets a personal script with his/her character's history and from then on improvises.
