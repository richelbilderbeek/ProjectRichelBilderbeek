  • 2008-2010: Secondary school teacher in biology, physics/chemistry and stagecraft: educating third and fourth year secondary school students from the levels of BBL, KBT and TL in the subjects of biology, physics/chemistry and stagecraft at VMBO 't Venster, Arnhem
