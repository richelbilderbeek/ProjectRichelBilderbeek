Courses and training

 

I give courses and training sessions in:

 

  • Arduino, weekly Dutch course at De Jonge Onderzoekers Groningen for every skill level
  • Maker course, weekly Dutch course at De Jonge Onderzoekers Groningen for every skill level
  • C++, irregularly, for beginners and more experienced programmers
