Heyo

 

In this song, K3 describes about mutual love. The verses are cheerful, the chorus is cheerful as well.

 

Personally, I love this song! In other reviews I write I dislike the use jabbertalk ('Heyah mama' has no meaning) and I do think the chorus is repeated just a bit too often. But this is all outweighed by the cheerful and naughty lyrics. K3 portrays themselves as women that like to love and make love, without any form of complexity. This is in my opinion an important part of the K3 philosophy; to make life as simple and full of love as possible. I'd like think, that it would be great if all people were like this!
