(Levend Cluedo) Sprookje

 

Levend Cluedo 'Sprookje' was written by Martijn Hennink and Richel Bilderbeek at the 22nd of March 2000.
The opinions of the characters described do not reflect the authors' opinions. Any resemblance to persons living or dead is purely coincidental.
This Levend Cluedo script may be copied, modified and performed freely as long as the authors' names are mentioned. It is not allowed to distribute modified copies of the script without permission from the authors.

 

Sprookje is a Levend Cluedo situated in a fairytale forest. The (mostly stupid) characters are parodies on existing fairytales. Most humour is too cliche for adults, where some roles are not suitable for children. This script will not convince actors new to Levend Cluedo. Personally, it is my least favorite script.
