In this song, K3 states that men should be on guard, because otherwise they'll be seduced by the girls.

 

Personally, I think the lyrics are fine. I enjoy the expression 'lieve pantoffelheld' [1], although I guess this is chosen just because it rhymes. The cat sounds [2] are a bit too weird for my taste. I like the beat of the song. I do like that it is a song about seduction, but I'd like to have more naughty or witty lyrics.
